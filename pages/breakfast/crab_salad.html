Spicy Crab Salad

I don't put a whole-ass crab on top as pictured above. AI made that image so tbh, I dunno what the f is going on with that salad. Ours is gonna look better so get to preppin':

Ingredients:

  • Package Imitation Crab Stick
  • Mayo(I like Vegenaise or Hellman's Vegan)
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sriracha
  • Celery
  • Radish, any type you like

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Serve with seasoned rice, chopped celery and sliced radish
