Make Ahead Oatmeal

  • 2 cups Rolled Oats
  • 1 cup Steel Cut Oats
  • 7 cups Water
  1. Cook in the pressure cooker using the porridge button, and let cool
  2. Store in the fridge and flavor each serving according to what you feel like that day
  3. See flavor options below. No matter what flavor I like to top it with a little sweet cream

Some flavor options:

  • apricots, almonds, orange zest and honey
  • a few dashes of nutmeg and brown sugar
  • peaches, chopped candied ginger and maple syrup
  • blueberries, coconut sugar and hempseeds
  • lotus paste and chopped up dried plums
