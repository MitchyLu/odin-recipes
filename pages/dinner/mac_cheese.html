Best Vegan Mac & Cheese

Says my kids when he was about 8

  • Box of Cavatappi
  • Bay Leaves
  • Onion, fine dice
  • Red Bell Pepper, small dice
  • Hot Italian Beyond Sausage, chopped
  • 2 bags Vegan Shredded Cheese(I like Daiya)
  • Soymilk
  • Chives
  • Parsley
  • Tarragon
  • Thyme
  • Powdered Rosemary
  • Sage
  • Dill
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Ume Plum Vinegar
  1. Cook the Cavatappi in salted water with the bayleaves added
  2. Drain, remove bayleaves and toos in a bit of olive oil
  3. Saute onion, red bell pepper and sausage in Earth Balance(vegan margarine) and set aside
  4. To the pasta add the cheese, and soymilk. Heat over low flame, stirring constantly until cheese is melted
  5. Add the sausage mixture and all the rest of the ingredients. Mix and serve
