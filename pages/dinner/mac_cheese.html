Best Vegan Mac & Cheese

When he was about 8 my kid proclaimed this version of vegan mac & cheese I slapped together one night: BEST MAC & CHEESE NON-NEGOTIABLE. Not to brag but, what do expect from the Mom who won the 'Best Cooker' award years prior?

Ingredients:

  • Box of Cavatappi
  • Bay Leaves
  • Onion, fine dice
  • Red Bell Pepper, small dice
  • Hot Italian Beyond Sausage, chopped
  • 2 bags Vegan Shredded Cheese(I like Daiya)
  • Soymilk
  • Chives
  • Parsley
  • Tarragon
  • Thyme
  • Powdered Rosemary
  • Sage
  • Dill
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Ume Plum Vinegar

Steps:

  1. Cook the Cavatappi in salted water with the bay leaves added
  2. Drain, remove bay leaves and toss in a bit of olive oil
  3. Saute onion, red bell pepper and sausage in Earth Balance vegan margarine and set aside
  4. To the pasta add the cheese, and soymilk. Heat over low flame, stirring constantly until cheese is melted
  5. Add the sausage mixture and all the rest of the ingredients. Mix and serve
