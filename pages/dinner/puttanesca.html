Puttanesca

  • 2 T Capers
  • 2 T Olive Oil
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 sm Onion, minced
  • 2 oz Red Wine
  • 2 tsp Maple Syrup
  • 3 T Kalamata Olives, minced
  • 3 cups Tomato Sauce
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Fresh Parsley, Basil & Oregano, chopped
  • Salt, TT
  1. Heat all ingredients together in a large saucepan
  2. Simmer for 10-15min
  3. Serve over your favorite pasta or rice
