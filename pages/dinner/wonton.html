Wonton

I used to make them just like my Porpor taught me but then I realized that I can create my own recipe. Here's my current fav way to make wonton.

  • Wonton Skins
  • Beyond ground beef
  • Chinese Chives (I use a mix of yellow and green but either is fine), chopped
  • Waterchestnuts, chopped
  • Five Spice
  • Garlic Powder
  • Oyster Mushroom Sauce
  1. Mix together the Beyond, chives, waterchestnuts, spices and mushroom sauce
  2. Fold up your wontons
  3. Freeze the ones you don't want by putting them on a baking sheet in the freezer. When frozen, tranfer them to a ziplock bag
  4. Boil the wontons in lightly boiling water for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Serve in soup or slathered in your favorite chili oil

The soup I usually make is:

  • Lee Kum Kee Mushroom Bullion
  • Bok Choy or Yu Choy
  • Fu Juk(dried soysheet, aka Yuba)
  • Rehydrated Mushroom slices
