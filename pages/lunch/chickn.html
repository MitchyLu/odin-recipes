Vegan Chick'n Wrap

Super simple, super fast and keeps me from wolfing the entire contents of my snack bin because I forgot to eat lunch again

Ingredients:

  • Vegan Chickn' Patty, heated
  • Ezekiel Tortilla
  • Chopped Tomato and Lettuce
  • Thinly sliced pepperoncini
  • vegan Mayo
  • Mustard

Steps:

  1. Toast the tortilla on the stovetop. Directly over low flame on a gas stove, in a pan on an electric stovetop
  2. Assemble all the ingredients inside the toasted wrap and enjoy
