Vegan Chickn' Wrap

  • Vegan Chickn' Patty, heated
  • Ezekiel Tortilla
  • Chopped Tomato and Lettuce
  • Thinly sliced pepperoncini
  • vegan Mayo
  • Mustard
  1. Toast the tortilla on the stovetop. Directly over low flame on a gas stove, in a pan on an electric stove
  2. Assemble all the ingredients inside the toasted wrap and enjoy
