Chickpea Salad

  • 1 can Chickpeas, drained, rinsed and mashed a bit but still chunky
  • Vegan Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Carrots, tiny dice
  • Celery, tiny dice
  • Scallions, chopped
  • Fresh Parsley, chopped
  • Paprika
  • Celery Seed
  • Salt & Pepper, TT
  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Eat on a greed salad or use as sandwich or wrap filling
