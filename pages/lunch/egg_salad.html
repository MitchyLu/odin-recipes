Vegan Egg Salad

The recipe no one asked for. But I like it so here:

  • Firm Tofu, small cubes, steamed and cooled
  • Vegan Mayo
  • 1 can Great Northern beans, drained and rinsed
  • Mustard
  • Black Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Pepper
  • Fresh or Dried Dill
  1. Blend the beans, condiments and spices (minus the dill) in a food processor until smooth
  2. Mix the blended mixture with the cooled tofu cubes
  3. Srir in the dill
  4. Eat on a green salad or use as sandwich or wrap filling
