Banana Bread

  • 8 very ripe Bananas, mashed
  • 1 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 t vanilla extract
  • 2 1/3 cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1 1/2 t Baking Soda
  • 1 1/2 t Baking Powder
  • Cinnamon, as much as you like
  • 1 t Nutmeg
  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mix together banana, sugar and vanilla in a large bowl
  3. In a separate bowl whisk together the rest of the ingredients
  4. Mix dry ingrediens into the banana mixture being careful not to overmix
  5. Pour into a greased loaf pan and bake for 1 hour or until toothpick comes out clean
  6. I like to eat a slice with plain yogurt on top
