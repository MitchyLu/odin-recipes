Black Rice Pudding

I'll go a really long time without making this and then suddenly remember what a treat it is to have a batch in the fridge. It's time. Great for breakfast too

Ingredients:

  • Black Rice, cooked
  • Coconut Milk
  • Sweetener(I like to use sucanat but you can use your fav sweetener to taste)
  • Banana, med dice
  • Mango, med dice
  • Pineapple, med dice

Steps:

  1. Mix everything together when the rice is still warm
  2. Eat warm or cold
